Rishikesh, Uttarakhand
Rishikesh, known as the "Yoga Capital of the World," is a spiritual sanctuary in the Himalayas. The sacred Ganges River flows through this picturesque town, making it a hub for pilgrims, yogis, and adventure enthusiasts.
Best Time to Visit: October to March is the ideal period to visit Rishikesh. The pleasant weather during these months is perfect for exploring the serene ashrams, engaging in river rafting, and admiring the stunning landscapes.

Mornings and evenings are ideal for spiritual activities, while daytime is perfect for adventure sports and sightseeing.
