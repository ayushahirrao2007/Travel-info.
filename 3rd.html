Ajanta Caves: Timeless artistry carved into ancient rock.

Ajanta Caves, Maharashtra
Ajanta Caves, renowned for their ancient rock-cut monuments, narrate the rich history of Buddhist heritage. These 30 caves, dating back to the 2nd century BCE, are masterpieces of Buddhist religious art and attract history enthusiasts and spiritual seekers alike.
Best Time to Visit: The ideal time to explore the Ajanta Caves is from October to March. The pleasant weather during these months is perfect for delving into the intricate artistry and admiring the historical significance of the caves.
